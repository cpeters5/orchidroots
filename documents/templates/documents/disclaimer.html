Data

  • Taxonomy data: WCSP is the primary source of taxonomy data. Other sources such as IPNI, peer reviewed publications, or academic publications are added when a taxon does not exist in WCSP or when there are differences that cannot be rectified. All species naming follows International Code of Nomenclature for algae, fungi, and plants (ICN). The name of species and natural hybrids is uniquely defined by the combination of Genus, species, infraspecific, and author.
  • Man-made hybrid name: In OrchidRoots, a man-made hybrid is uniquely defined by their formula and its name follows the International Code of Nomenclature for Cultivated Plants (ICNCP). Our primary source of hybrid is the Royal Horticulture Society registration (RHS). In addition, unregistered hybrids are also included in OrchidRoots based on parentage given by users on the condition that the given parentage does not exist or is not a synonym of any registered parenage in RHS.
All data sources are given credit as due. In most cases names are displayed with a link to their original source if available. While we have taken precautions to ensure that the data contained in OrchidRoots website has been obtained from reliable sources and is both current and accurate, errors may happen from time to time.
